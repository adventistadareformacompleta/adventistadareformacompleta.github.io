THE PERSECUTOR TRIPLE ALLIANCE

There will be an alliance of powers between the dragon, the beast and the false prophet, with the aim of stimulating the State, that is, the civil power to formulate persecuting laws against the church of God. The United States, the beast with two horns, represents apostate Protestantism, from this nation will come the decree imposing Sunday observance. The three deceiving powers unite with each other and demand from the kings of the earth, that is, from the civil powers, the contest for the final battle against God, his people and the truth. These powers are the three frog spirits. "Then I saw three unclean spirits like frogs come out of the mouth of the dragon, out of the mouth of the beast, and out of the mouth of the false prophet; for they are spirits of demons, working signs, and they go to the kings of the whole world to gather them to the battle of the great day of God Almighty" Revelation 16:13-14 .

What do these powers represent? The dragon is Satan ( Revelation 12:9 ) working through spiritism. Spiritualism is used by Satan in four different ways:

THE PAGANISM

Pagan forms of worship and superstition dominated by spiritism. And falsehoods borrowed from paganism, incorporated into Christianity.

MODERN AND SOCIAL SPIRITISM

The belief that the spirits of the dead return to minister to the living. Social spiritism, carrying out many social works.

CHRISTIAN SPIRITISM

Which develops in evangelical and Catholic cults through healings, miracles and prosperity based on the doctrines of the immortality of the soul and the sanctification of Sunday.

SCIENTIFIC SPIRITISM

That propagates in the academic environment with the practice of research in the laboratory, bearing the flag of parapsychology and other modern designations.

The beast is Satan acting through the papacy ( Revelation 13:1-13 ) as a false prophet, Satan acts in the apostate evangelical churches by refusing the truth, receiving support from the State. According to White, The Great Controversy.

In Chapter 13:1-10, the beast "like a leopard" is described, to which the dragon gave "his power, his throne, and great might." This symbol, as most Protestants have believed, represents the papacy, which succeeded to the power, throne and might once held by the ancient Roman Empire.
Ellen G. White, 1985, The Great Controversy, page 474

The renowned author continues her narration by pointing out two common mistakes shared by Catholics and Evangelicals.

Satan, through the three spirits of frogs will persecute the people of God, even on pain of death, these three unclean spirits will encourage the American government to enact the decree imposing Sunday observance, with serious departure from democratic principles and religious freedom .
