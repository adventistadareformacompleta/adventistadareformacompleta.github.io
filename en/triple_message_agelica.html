The Third Angel's message is the righteousness of Christ , when the church receives, brings communion with God and with each other preparing it for the hour of judgment, this message must be preached and lived by God's people as a trumpet.

"And I saw another angel fly in the midst of heaven, having the everlasting gospel to proclaim to them that dwell on the earth, and to every nation and tribe and tongue and people, saying with a loud voice, Fear God and give Him glory, for the hour of His judgment is come. And worship Him who made heaven, and earth, and the sea, and fountains of water" Revelation 14:6-7 .

The first angel's message of Revelation 14, heralding the hour of God's judgment and appealing to men to fear and worship Him, was designed to separate God's professing people from the corrupting influences of the world, and to awaken them to to see their true state of worldliness and apostasy. God sent to the church, in this message, a warning which, if accepted, would have corrected the evils which were separating it from Him. The church would once again have attained the blessed state of unity, faith, and love, which there was in the apostolic days, when "the heart and soul were one" of the believers, and "they boldly proclaimed the Word of God", days when "the LORD added to the church those who were to be saved.

THE MESSAGE FROM THE SECOND ANGEL

This message was proclaimed along with the first angel's message and invites God's people to come out of the various religious denominations that have been drinking the wine of Babylonian doctrine. It will be proclaimed again, and the servants of God who are in these denominations corrupted by apostasy, will heed the call to preach the church of God founded on the righteousness of Christ , and join the church of God. A hard test awaits the Lord's people with persecution, imprisonment and death, however, the church will bravely resist until the victory of the Latter Rain . The Lord has servants in these denominations, and they will be drawn by the last invitation of the angel of Revelation 18.

"After these things I saw another angel come down from heaven, having great authority, and the earth was lightened with his glory. Then he cried with a mighty voice, saying, "Fallen! den of every unclean spirit, and a hiding place of every unclean and detestable bird. I heard another voice from heaven, saying, Come out of her, my people, that ye be not partakers of her sins, and that ye do not partake of her plagues." Revelation 18:1-2;4 .

In the eighteenth chapter of Revelation, God's people are invited to leave Babylon. According to this passage, many of God's people must still be in Babylon. And in what religious bodies will most of the followers of Christ find themselves today? Undoubtedly, in the various churches that profess the Protestant faith.
Ellen G. White, 1994, Final Events, page 170

In fact, the angel of Revelation 18 joins the third angel of Revelation 14 in proclaiming the final message of God's mercy to the world (the 3rd angel's message is the receiving of Christ's righteousness to obey God's law), this message is a repetition of the second Angel's message of Revelation 14:8, which will be rejected.

"Another angel followed, the second, saying, Babylon the great is fallen, is fallen, which made all nations drink of the wine of the fury of her fornication" Revelation 14:8 .

"[She] has made all nations drink the wine of the fury of her fornication." Revelation 14:6-8. How is this effected? Forcing men to accept a spurious Sabbath. However, it cannot yet be said that... "she made all nations drink of the wine of the wrath of her fornication". She has not yet given all nations a drink.
The fall of Babylon will be completed when this condition is reached, and the union of the church with the world has been consummated throughout Christendom. The change is gradual, and the perfect fulfillment of Revelation 14:8 is still in the future. When your sins pile up to heaven? Revelation 18:2-5. When the law of God is finally made void by legislation.
Ellen G. White, 1994, Final Events, page 171

THE MESSAGE FROM THE THIRD ANGEL

The sealing is a process that will take place in the church of God. When Jesus in 1844 closed one door (first apartment of the sanctuary) and opened another (of the second apartment), He sent to the world an angel with the third angel's message, whose message reveals the mystery covered by the gospel, namely, the righteousness of Christ. . The sinner has a sinful nature, is inclined to evil, Jesus will cover the penitent sinner with the divine nature , His righteousness. However, only those who accept the third angel's message will be contemplated, surviving the shaking and the time of trouble , receiving the refreshment of the latter rain .and will be sealed forever. We must direct our attention to the work of Christ in the heavenly sanctuary .

"He said unto me, Until two thousand three hundred evenings and mornings; and the sanctuary shall be cleansed" Daniel 8:14 .

When Jesus' ministry in the holy place was ended, and he had passed into the most holy place and stood before the ark, which contained the law of God, he sent another mighty angel with a third message to the world. A scroll was placed in the angel's hand, and, as he descended to earth in power and majesty, he proclaimed a stern warning, with the most terrible threat ever made to man. This message was designed to put the children of God on their guard, showing them the hour of temptation and anguish that lay before them. Said the angel: "They will be brought into close combat with the beast and his image. Their only hope of eternal life is to stand fast. Since their lives are at stake, they must hold fast to the truth." The third angel ends his message thus: "Here is the patience of the saints;
Ellen G. White, 1988, Early Writings, page 254

Despite the efforts of this third angel, the message was rejected in 1888 and continues to be rejected in these last days, implying terrible consequences for the wicked, since the servants of God who accept the message will be contemplated with the sealing confirming the righteousness of Christ in their hearts forever, were refreshed. The results sculpted here confirm the veracity of the word, as the way the message is received, result in distinct consequences.

"And after these things I saw another angel come down from heaven, having great power, and the earth was lightened with his glory" Revelation 18:1 .
